Welcome to hybrix! hybrix is a highly inclusive distributed platform environment to network and manage digital forms of value transfer, interlinking other decentralized networks. It solves the problem of a currently fragmented ecosystem of solutions in digital ledger technologies and alternative economies. The hybrix network runs through a collection of decentralized nodes and puts a lot of power at your fingertips.
